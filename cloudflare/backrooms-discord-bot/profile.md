1️⃣ Profile Features to Include
Basic Info
Discord username & avatar
Level & total XP
Progress toward next level (e.g., progress bar or percentage)
Trial Progress
List of Wander Trials attempted
Challenges completed vs remaining
Scenario participation and full completion badges
Optional: highlight recent trials
Achievements / Badges
List of unlocked achievements
Visual badges/icons (can be emojis or images)
Optionally, achievements still locked (dimmed or “?”)
Statistics
Total trials completed
Total challenges completed
XP earned this week / monthly
Optional: streaks for daily/weekly challenges
